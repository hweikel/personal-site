I print & sell books through Dancing Star Press, send rare dispatches from periods of shore leave, convene rarer-still meetings of the Late Night Late Plays Reading Group, and live most often in exile from fair Ladonia.

Once, I maintained starlight convenience, took notes on pebbledash, overstayed at the Barbican, translated scraps of The Strange Creature from the North (Swedish) and stanzas from Anne (Norwegian).
